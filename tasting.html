Our Space

Here at Sunrise we love both good beer and a good time. Come spend the afternoon with family and friends in our fully-licensed brewery where we serve up the best snacks to bring out the full flavours of our beers. We offer both daily and private bookings of our tasting room.

On Tap

We are proud to serve only the finest in local ingredients as well as support our local farmers.

We offer exclusive new brews in our tasting room. Come try our latest delicious experiments.

From crisp lagers to hoppy IPAs and the darkest stouts, everyone leaves with a favourite.

Private Bookings

While everyone is welcome in our establishment 7 days a week, we also offer private bookings for both smaller parties as well as larger corporate functions.

Our tasting tour is the true Sunrise experience and we are able to accommodate up to 30 people at once, and host multiple tours daily. We can also host groups as small as 4 people if you're looking for that perfect Friday night, double-date outing. Our staff is passionate about sharing their knowledge, so feel free to get in touch with us about any private events we can help you with.
