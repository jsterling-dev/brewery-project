Our Space

Here at Sunrise we love both good beer & good times! Our brewery hosts 2 amazing tasting experiences. Come spend the afternoon with family and friends in our fully licensed restaurant, where we serve up the best dishes to bring out the full flavours of our beers. We also have a tasting room where we offer both daily and private bookings.

On Tap

We are proud to serve only the finest in local ingredients as well support our local farmers.

We offer exclusive new brews in our tasting room. Come try our latest delicious experiments.

From crisp lagers to hoppy IPAs and the darkest stouts, we offer every beer imaginable. Everyone leaves with a favorite!

Private Bookings

While anyone is welcome in our establishment 7 days a week, we also offer private bookings for both smaller parties as well as larger corporate functions!

Our tasting tour is the true Sunrise experience and we are able to accomadate up to 30 people at once, with multiple tours happening daily. We can also host groups as small as 4 people if you're looking for that perfect Friday night double date outting. Our staff is passionate about sharing their knowledge, so get in touch with us about any private events we can help you with!
